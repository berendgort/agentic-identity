# LinkedIn Connection Message Templates

Quick templates for connecting with different people at target companies (200 char limit).

---

## üéØ General Principles

- **Keep it under 200 characters** (LinkedIn limit)
- **Be specific** about shared interests/work
- **No obvious job hunting** ("not applying now" or skip entirely)
- **Show credibility** quickly (PhD, Triton, evaluation, etc.)
- **Natural tone** like you're actually typing, not using a template

## üö´ What NOT to Say

**Avoid:**
- ‚ùå "I'm looking for opportunities"
- ‚ùå "I'm interested in joining [Company]"
- ‚ùå "Open to work"
- ‚ùå "Would love to discuss opportunities"
- ‚ùå "I'd be a great fit for your team"
- ‚ùå Anything that sounds desperate or templated
- ‚ùå Long paragraphs of your accomplishments

**Instead:**
- ‚úÖ "Would be good/great to connect"
- ‚úÖ "Would love to connect"
- ‚úÖ "Always good to connect with folks working on [X]"
- ‚úÖ Keep it casual and natural
- ‚úÖ Show genuine interest in their work

**Character count rule:** 150-180 chars is the sweet spot.

---

## ‚ö° Quick Tips

1. **Research first** - Check their profile for 30 seconds
2. **Find common ground** - Shared tech, interests, or goals
3. **Be specific** - "LLM evaluation" not "AI stuff"
4. **Stay casual** - Like texting a colleague, not writing a cover letter
5. **No pressure** - Just connecting, no ask
6. **Respond quickly** - If they accept, say thanks within 24h

## üìÖ Connection Strategy

### Weekly Target
- 5-10 new connections at target companies
- Mix of roles: 50% engineers, 30% recruiters, 20% researchers
- Spread across companies (not all at one company at once)

### Natural Cadence
- Monday: 2-3 connections
- Wednesday: 2-3 connections  
- Friday: 1-2 connections
- Don't connect with 20 people at once (looks desperate)
